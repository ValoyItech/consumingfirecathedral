About Our Ministry

Consuming Fire Cathedral is a Spirit-filled church established to bring the fire of God's presence to South Africa and beyond. We are a community of believers passionate about worship, prayer, and seeing lives transformed by the power of Jesus Christ.

Senior Prophet & Founder

"Carrying the mantle of fire to nations."

A man of God with a burning passion for souls and a heart for revival. Prophet Musa carries a powerful anointing for healing, deliverance, and prophetic ministry.

Prophetess Fikile Baloyi

Intercessory Leader

"A voice of prayer, healing, and deliverance."

A powerful woman of prayer with a heart for intercession and healing. Prophetess Fikile leads our prayer ministry and women's outreach programs.
